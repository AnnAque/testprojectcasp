What is Project CASP?

                       

Project CASP is an organization aiming to make our world clean and a sustainable Place for living hence
the name. Our world is experiencing a lot of issues mainly caused by us humans, and it is only right for
us to fix the problems we caused. Through project CASP you can join or plan events that can help sustain
and maintain our world clean and green. If interested click here to sign up!
